CAUSE & EFFECT

Master the Stack, manipulate your Sequence, and outsmart your opponents.

How to Win

Your goal is to build a specific Sequence of cards on your board. You win if you achieve:

4 of a Kind

Have 4 copies of the exact same card type in your sequence.

The Full Set

Have exactly 1 copy of all 5 different card types.

Pro-tip: If you try to draw from an empty deck, you also win!

Turn Phases

Each turn follows a strict 4-phase cycle:

  • 1. Draw: Pick up one card.
  • 2. Cause: Play 1 card into your Sequence.
  • 3. React: Opponents may respond from their hand.
  • 4. Resolve: Actions on the Stack take effect.

The Stack (LIFO)

When you play a card, it doesn't happen immediately! It goes on The Stack.

Opponents can "React" by discarding cards from their hand to add their own effects on top.

Last-In, First-Out: The last reaction played is the first one to resolve. Use this to counter or steal cards before they resolve!

The 5 Card Types

Every card has a Sequence ability (when played) and a React ability (when responding).

Stagnation: Draw or Counter.
Vacuum: Pressure opponent's hand.
Reflection: Return cards to hand.
Stasis: Manipulate the deck/stack.
Assimilation: Destroy or Steal cards.
