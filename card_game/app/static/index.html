CAUSE & EFFECT

A highly technical 2-4 player card game about priority, reaction, and careful sequencing.

OR JOIN BY CODE

HOW TO PLAY

THE GOAL

Build a Sequence of cards on your board. You win if you achieve:

4 OF A KIND

4 copies of the exact same card type in your sequence.

THE FULL SET

Exactly 1 copy of all 5 different card types.

TURN PHASES

  • 1. DRAW: Pick up one card.
  • 2. CAUSE: Play 1 card into your Sequence.
  • 3. REACT: Opponents may respond from hand by discarding.
  • 4. RESOLVE: The Stack resolves in Last-In, First-Out order.

CARD TYPES

STAGNATION: Draw / Counter placement
VACUUM: Multi-pitch / Steal from hand
REFLECTION: Return card to owner's hand
STASIS: Put card on top of deck
ASSIMILATION: Destroy or Steal card
